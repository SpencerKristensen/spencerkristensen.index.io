Spencer Biography

Spencer was born on November 27th, 1998 to Remi and Kathryn Kristensen. Spencer was raised in Pueblo, Colorado, and grew up playing hockey and soccer. He is a self proclaimed film and music enthusiast, and spends his freetime skateboarding, being with friends, and doing a variety of other hobbies. He has an older sister named Sydney, who works as a tech mediary in Denver, Colorado. Spencer completed his high school education in Pueblo, Colorado from East High School in 2017. He was considering attending Penn State to play soccer, but decided that his future lies in the entertainment industry. He moved to LA in 2017 and was there for nearly two years. He returned to Pueblo in 2019, and enrolled at Colorado State University Pueblo. He currently studies digital media production, and audio production. Upon graduation he plans to move to Albuquerque, and attempt to build his resume in the world of film. His ambition is to become a director and writer.
